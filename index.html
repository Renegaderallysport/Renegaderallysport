About Our Team

Renegade Rallysport is a dedicated rally team competing in the American Rally Association's Limited Two Wheel Drive class. We bring passion, precision, and relentless determination to every stage.

Our team represents the spirit of grassroots motorsport, combining technical expertise with the raw excitement of rally racing. From forest stages to desert trails, we push our machines and ourselves to the absolute limit.

Follow our journey as we tackle the most challenging rally events across America, always striving for that perfect balance of speed and control that defines championship rally driving.
